# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 21, 2026
**Version:** 1.0

Detooz ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our scam detection service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number (encrypted).
- **Profile Data:** Country code, language preference.

### 1.2 Scam Detection Data
- **Message Content:** Text, URLs, and phone numbers you submit for scanning.
- **Metadata:** Sender information, timestamp.
- **App Analytics:** Usage patterns, feature interaction (with consent).

## 2. How We Use Your Data

### 2.1 Core Service
- To scan messages for potential scams and fraud.
- To alert your designated guardians (if feature enabled).
- To maintain your trusted sender list.

### 2.2 Improvement & Research (With Consent Only)
- **Training Data:** If you opt-in, we use anonymized message content to train our AI models to better detect new scams.
- **Pattern Analysis:** To identify emerging scam trends across the community.

## 3. Training Data & Anonymization

If you consent to share data for AI training:
- We remove personally identifiable information (PII) like names, specific account numbers, and personal addresses before storage.
- Anonymized data helps protect all users by improving our detection algorithms.
- **You can opt-out at any time** in Privacy Settings. Opting out stops future collection and anonymizes previously contributed data.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR & Indian DPDP Act)

You have the following rights regarding your data:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
- **Right to Correction:** Update inaccurate personal information.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your account and associated data.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Revoke permission for training data or analytics at any time.
- **Right to Portability:** Export your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, use the "Privacy & Data" section in the app settings.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained as long as your account is active.
- **Scan History:** Retained for 365 days by default (adjustable in settings).
- **Training Data:** Anonymized patterns are retained indefinitely for model improvement unless you request removal.

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- **Encryption:** All data in transit and sensitive data at rest (like passwords) is encrypted.
- **Access Control:** Strict limitations on who can access data.
- **Audit Logs:** Tracking consent changes and data access.
