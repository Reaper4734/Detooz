# User Consent Policy

**Effective Date:** January 21, 2026
**Version:** 1.0

This policy outlines how Detooz requests, manages, and honors your consent regarding your personal data.

## 1. What is Consent?

Consent is your voluntary, specific, informed, and unambiguous agreement to the processing of your personal data. At Detooz, we believe you should always be in control of your information.

## 2. Types of Consent We Request

### 2.1 Essential Service (Terms of Service)
By creating an account, you agree to our Terms of Service which allows us to:
- Process your login credentials.
- Scan messages you explicitly submit.
- Store your preferences.

### 2.2 Optional: Training Data Contribution
You may choose to help us improve our AI models.
- **What you agree to:** Allowing us to use anonymized versions of confirmed scam messages to train our system.
- **Benefits:** Helps the entire community stay safe from new scam patterns.
- **Privacy:** All personal identifiers are removed before use.

### 2.3 Optional: Usage Analytics
You may choose to share how you use the app.
- **What you agree to:** Sharing anonymous data about which features you use and how often.
- **Benefits:** Helps us prioritize new features and improvements.

## 3. How to Give Consent

- **During Onboarding:** You will be presented with clear options to opt-in to optional data collection.
- **In Settings:** You can review and change your choices at any time in `Settings > Privacy & Data`.

## 4. How to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time without penalty.

1. Go to `Settings > Privacy & Data`.
2. Toggle off "Share Training Data" or "Share Analytics".
3. Your choice takes effect immediately.

**Impact of Withdrawal:**
- We will stop collecting new data for that purpose.
- Providing the core scam detection service will NOT be affected.
- You can continue to use the app normally.

## 5. Minors

Detooz is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without verified parental consent.

## 6. Updates to Policy

If we make significant changes to how we use your data, we will:
1. Notify you via email or in-app notification.
2. Ask you to review the new policy.
3. Request your consent again if the changes affect your privacy rights.
